


# 句子和句型




## 句子类型





## 基本句型
* 主语 + 谓语（不及物动词）+ [状语]
    * People  work
    * It  happens  (often)
    * The method   works   (effectively)
* 主语 + 谓语 + 宾语
    * I  love  you
    * He  knows  everything
    * I  caught   a cold
* 主语 + 谓语 + 间接宾语 + 直接宾语
    * 通常人做间接宾语
    * He   passed   me   the book
    * He   teaches   me   English
    * I   offered  the candidate   my support
* 主语  + 系动词 + 表语
    * I   am    Chinese
    * Out house   is  clean
    * The president   seemed    pleasant
* 主语 + 谓语 + 宾语 + 宾语补足语
    * She   made    me    happy
    * The girl    thinks    herself    intelligent
    * I    found    the game    interesting
